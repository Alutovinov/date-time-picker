Watching changes of selected year and selected month

When a year or month is selected from 'multi-years' and 'year' views, the yearSelected() and monthSelected() outputs emit a date representing the chosen year or month. The dates representing years will have their month set to January and their day set to the 1st. Dates representing months will have their day set to the 1st of the month.

Beware that the emitted value does not affect the current value in the connected input, which is only bound to the selection made in the 'month' view. So if the end user closes the calendar after choosing a year in multi-view mode (by pressing the ESC key, for example), the selected year, emitted by yearSelected output, will not cause any change in the value of the date in the associated input.
